What is AcmeClaw

AI Agents That Serve,
Not Deflect

AcmeClaw is a consultancy and platform born from the conviction that the customer is the nucleus around which everything must orbit. We design AI-powered customer experience architectures that turn every interaction into an act of genuine obsession.

Drawing from deep expertise in AWS, GenAI, and contact center transformation, AcmeClaw helps organizations move beyond chatbot theater to build intelligent, anticipatory systems that understand context, remember history, and take real action on behalf of customers.

Inspired by the open-source AI agent movement, AcmeClaw bridges the gap between cutting-edge agent orchestration and enterprise-grade customer experience — no vendor lock-in, no black boxes, no deflection.
